Before you begin

Tell the story of your provision through the Artsmark Framework

This application asks you to describe your arts and cultural practice against each of the eight Artsmark Framework criteria.

For each criterion, you will be asked what practice currently looks like, how it is embedded, what difference it makes and what you are learning or strengthening next.

Assessors are interested in concrete, reflective accounts rather than polished claims. You do not need to describe everything. Choose examples that help someone understand the quality, intention and impact of your practice.

Focus on what helps an assessor understand your practice

Use concrete examples to explain what happens, why it matters, what difference it makes and how you know.

You do not need to describe everything. It is useful to be open about challenges, differences across the setting and anything that remains fragile or is still developing.
