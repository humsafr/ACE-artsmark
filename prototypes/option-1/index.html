Option 1: Guided reflection

This prototype explores a conversational, step-by-step approach to assessment.

Schools would be guided through a sequence of reflective questions, helping them describe their current practice, identify strengths and choose areas for development.

What this option is testing

  • Whether a guided journey makes the framework easier to understand.
  • Whether schools can reflect meaningfully without writing long statements.
  • Whether prompts can help schools connect activity, intention and impact.
  • Whether the experience feels supportive while still enabling assessment.
