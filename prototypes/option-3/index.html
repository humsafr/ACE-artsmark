Before you begin

Build a picture of your arts and cultural provision

This questionnaire helps you describe how arts, culture and creativity currently work across your school or setting.

You will respond to three questions under each of the eight Artsmark Framework criteria. Choose the answer that feels closest to your setting today.

There may not be a perfect fit. Select the best approximate answer. You will be able to review and change your responses before seeing your snapshot.

This snapshot is not the assessment itself

Your answers create a descriptive picture of relative strengths and areas that may be less developed. They do not determine an Artsmark award level.

After viewing the snapshot, you will be asked to add context, examples and evidence so an assessor can understand what sits behind your choices.
