Option 3: Evidence-led assessment

This prototype explores an assessment built around examples of practice, evidence and change over time.

Instead of relying mainly on long written statements, schools would provide a smaller number of focused examples showing what they did, why they did it, what changed and how they know.

What this option is testing

  • Whether examples provide a more credible picture of practice.
  • Whether schools can show impact without producing lengthy narratives.
  • Whether different forms of evidence can support fair assessment.
  • Whether this approach better connects planning, action and reflection.
