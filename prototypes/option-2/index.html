Before you begin

Tell a coherent story about your arts and cultural provision

This version brings the eight Artsmark Framework criteria together into four connected narrative sections.

Each section asks two substantial questions. The prompts help you describe current practice, give examples, explain impact, and be open about challenges or areas that remain less developed.

The framework criteria remain visible throughout, but you can respond through a more joined-up account of how the work operates across your setting.

What you'll be asked to provide

You'll give long-form answers across four sections. At the end, you'll also have the option to add one short video of up to five minutes to show or explain something that is difficult to convey in writing. The video is optional and production quality is not assessed.

      Optional recorded contribution

      Bring one important part of your application to life

      You can add one short video to show or explain something that is difficult to convey fully in writing.

      This is optional. Choosing not to add a video will not disadvantage your application.

      The prompt

      What would you like the assessor to understand more clearly?

      Use your video to add insight into one aspect of your provision.

      This might include:

      • children and young people describing their experience or influence
      • a senior leader explaining how arts and culture are embedded
      • a concrete example of practice or impact
      • something about your context that helps explain your written answers

      Do not use the video simply to repeat the written application.

      Add your video

      Accepted formats: MP4, MOV or WebM. Maximum five minutes. File-size limit to be confirmed.

      • One video only

      What assessors will consider

      Assessors will consider only the insight the video adds about:

      • context
      • practice
      • voice
      • impact
      • embeddedness

      Production quality will not be assessed.

      Before uploading

      • Make sure appropriate consent is in place
      • Include captions or a transcript
      • Avoid including unnecessary personal information
