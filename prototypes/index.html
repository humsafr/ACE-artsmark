Why we are testing

Help us shape a fair and useful assessment process

We are exploring three possible ways for schools to describe their arts and cultural provision.

The content is still draft and will change. These prototypes give us something tangible to respond to, rather than asking everyone to begin from scratch.

The aim is not to select one “best” option. We want to understand which formats and elements are useful, and what could be combined into a process worth testing further.

We will also test these approaches with schools to understand where they experience ease, confidence, clarity or confusion.

About these prototypes

All text is draft and do not represent the final Artsmark assessment.

Nothing is submitted or stored remotely. The wording, questions and interactions are all open to change.
