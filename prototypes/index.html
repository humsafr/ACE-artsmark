Artsmark assessment prototypes

These prototypes explore three possible approaches to the redesigned Artsmark assessment experience.

Each option is intended to test a different balance between reflection, assessment rigour, usability and the quality of evidence schools can provide.

Prototype options

Option 1: Guided reflection

A conversational, step-by-step assessment that helps schools reflect on their practice and build a rounded picture across the Artsmark framework.

Option 2: Structured self-assessment

A more structured assessment using multiple-choice questions, indicators and short explanations across all eight framework criteria.

Option 3: Evidence-led assessment

An assessment centred on examples, evidence and change over time, helping schools show what is happening in practice rather than relying primarily on long written statements.

About the prototypes

These are early prototypes for testing ideas, not finished designs. Their purpose is to help the Artsmark team, assessors and schools explore what feels clear, useful, credible and manageable.
